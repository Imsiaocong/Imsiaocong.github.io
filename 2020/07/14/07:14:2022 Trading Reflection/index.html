07/14/2022 Trading Reflection

Today’s market started at red area where all three indices traded lower than previous close due to last night’s crash globally. Since the opening, the uptrend has continued.

In the morning, there were a flash crash in the first 30 minutes from opening. I took the chance and bought a $AAPL July 24 400c as I believed it was the bottom for today. Luckily I was right and the stock traded higher so I was able to cash out with a profit of 40 dollars.

I also bought 2 shares of $DOCU as I believed the stock is trading at a week-low price and is about to go up. So far my bet is correct and I am profiting from DOCU. I believe these cloud stock will continue their momentum and is a great choice for COVID-19 stocks portfolio.

Overall, I expect a very fluctuated market during Q2 Earning season. Thus, rational investing strategy is crucial for a good performance during this time. I would continue to reduce my trade each day if the situation does continue. I would also keep an eye on VIX for inspecting the volatility and personal outlook of the market.
