@Trading Reflection July 13th, 2020

Today’s market starts green and continued to go higher until sometime around 1p.m. Dow Jones was obviously more bullish than the other two indices, possibly due to lack of performance in its comprised industries such as banking and airlines which performed well today.

I chose not to trade in the morning because I had class from 9 to 11 and was unable to find a particularly promising trade. Was originally planning to do a call on $JPM$ for its earning tomorrow which is believed to be somewhat bullish. However, I let go the chance due to uncertainty in the morning.

As the market start to crash in the afternoon, I start to think about potential trade. I am eager to do some options trading so I did long strangle on JPM in the afternoon which turned out to be on a bearish and I lost money on the call for roughly 30 dollars which was compensated by the puts for about 28 dollars so I had total lose of 2 dollars in this trade. I sold these positions because the worry of further downside.

The second trade I did was a covered call on $NIO$ as I thought the stock has gone down a lot and had the potential to go up. I did 16c and shorted 25 of its stocks. However the result was it kept going down so I lost a total of around 8 dollars despite the hedge from the short position.

Today’s last trade I did was on $DOCU$ and $SE$. From my understanding, the cloud stocks have too much momentum that it is just hard to stop from keeping going up. Plus the drop for DOCU was a lot which made me even more confident on opening a long position on DOCU. Same for SE which had been trading around 120 for days and as it dropped to 113, I felt there is decent amount of safety margin to buy some shares.
