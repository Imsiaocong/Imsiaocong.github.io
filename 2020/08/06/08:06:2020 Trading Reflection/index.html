08:06:2020 Trading Reflection

Yeeeeeeeeeeeet
2020-08-06

I definitely learned a lot this week, at least comparing to the previous weeks.

After FOMO of missing out the earnings last week, I decided to play some earnings this week. Ant this decision lead to both the good and the bad.

The bad thing is I lost 300 bucks over night just for the two weekly contracts I bought. I haven’t really experience IV crash befor so I did not take ATVI’s 140% IV in to consideration when buying it (which I totally should).

As a result, despite all the beats on ATVI’s earning, the stock crashed from 87 dollars per share to 84 dps. Plus the drop in IV, I lost nearly 60% of the premium.

After loss in ATVI weekly call, I sold these two calls and bought a 9/18 90c as I learned IV crash and how OTM month-out option can save your premium from IV crash.

As of now, this 9/18 90c has made 50% return and I am still holding it as I see 90 is more than achievable before 9/18!

I also traded BABA call options as I identified there was a gap to be filled in BABA’s price action plus the unusual option activity in BABA. A quick 60 buck was made.
