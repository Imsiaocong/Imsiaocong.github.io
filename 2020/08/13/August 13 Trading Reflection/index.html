August 13 Trading Reflection

Yeeeeeeeeeeeet
2020-08-13

August 13, indices futures were trading flat last night probably affected by oversea markets in Asia and Europe. We started today with not much up across all indices.

In the morning, I specifically focused on trading SE which I saw a chance of the stock gapping up today from technical analysis. IV Rank has been dropping since yesterday’s peak, which is pretty bullish.

TSLA has been trending higher since the start of the split news and I am glad that I bought more stock the second I saw the news. I have noticed a uptrend in its short interest which is bullish, we can easily see TSLA goes up more once the next wave of short squeeze starts.

For options, I am currently holding my cash secured puts on AMD as I believe, firstly, AMD has a strong support at 77 dps, and its IV Rank has only one way to go from now which is downward. Thus I am confident to hold my cash secured puts longer.

AAPL has been doing surprisingly well today but since I had an interview, I was unable to catch this chance. I have seen large unusual activity on AAPL afterwards. I really wish I can do a call spread on AAPL because since it has broken the ATH, there is no way but up for this awesome stock. At least, AAPL is my heaviest holding (23.87%) and I still have great profit from the shares. Also, today is the divident pay day so I guess that is one of the reason it is trending higher.
