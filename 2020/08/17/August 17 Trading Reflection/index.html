August 17 Trading Reflection

Yeeeeeeeeeeeet
2020-08-17

I want to use this post as a chance to standardize the format of this blog. First, it will start with the ‘Stock in Focus’ section where I will record 5 stocks with the most unusual buying volumn during trading hours. After that I will write about today’s major indices (probably NASDQ lol because other indices are boring) followed by my trades in the day.

Stock in Focus

  • JD - 29.6% Surprise on earning and the stock went up significantly during PM but was unable to recover from the morning selloff until noon.

  • RGEN - Probably the most unnoticed winner today. With a 3.6M 8/17 145c order, the stock did went up since the start of the morning.

  • TSLA - Strong momentum from the futures last night and it continued to broke the 1800 ressistance line. Next stop 2000!

  • NVDA - Same momentum also favored NVDA as we are about to have its earning this week.

  • GLD - Trended up today, probably related to Buffett’s bet on Gold. Oh well.

  • GD - No comment

Major Indices

NASDAQ ATH again, while Dow got back to where it should be. In general, my overview of the market is I will keep betting on tech while taking look at traditional business. But at the same time, as Cathie Wood said, technologies has deeply integrated with all kinds of businesses. So if a traditional business is going up, it has to bring some tech industries up as well.

My Trades

Today is a selling day, sold 1 SE 8/21 110p for the 130 dollars of premium. As earnings approaching, SE is having a IV Rank level too high not to sell options for the premiu. As I have a positive outlook for the company, I decided to sell this put.

I also sold 3 shares of SPY etf. I bought them back in March’s crash. However, I am not satisfied with the return so I decided to sell some of it and put it somewhere else.
