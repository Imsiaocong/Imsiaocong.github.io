August 11 Trading Reflection

Yeeeeeeeeeeeet
2020-08-12

Tuesday was not the best day for bulls for sure.

In my experience, we get this kind of sell of every single month. In my mind, I believe it is just another monthly selloff because:

  • We have VIX trending down for more than two weeks as of yesterday.

  • The crash down was not as dramatic as in the march at all.

  • Too many new comers who literally just joined within two weeks started to think ‘this is the end of the world’.

These reasons are simple but people would still doubt if we were heading into a bear market because of the fear of losing money (which is why I believe it is so important to implement automated trading eventually where human emotion would not be involved.)

For me, even though I held up my stocks strong, I still had not been able to held ‘diamond hand’ on my options. Which caused me more than 600 dollars of loss within two days. On the brightside, because I always keep the risk premium within the boundary I would accept, I am alright with this kind of loss.

However, as I noticed that I am still lack of sufficient ‘mental strength’ in options trading. I decided to write put options on stocks like AMD instead for monthly premium gain. I plan to strict follow this for two months and cultivate my mental strength at the mean time.
